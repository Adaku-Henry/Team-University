Our Journey

Our History

Benchmark Business and Technical College was established to bridge gaps in business, vocational, and technical education through practical learning, technological advancement, and industry-oriented training.

Foundation

The institution was founded with a mission to provide affordable, practical, and career-focused education.

Program Growth

The college expanded its academic and technical programs to meet growing market and community needs.

Skills Development

Stronger emphasis was placed on practical training, entrepreneurship, innovation, and employability.

Community Impact

The college continues to support youth, women, and communities through inclusive education and capacity-building programs.

Our Vision

To become a leading center of excellence in business and technical education, innovation, research, and ethical professional training that contributes to sustainable development.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, practical, and affordable business and technical education that empowers learners with knowledge, skills, and entrepreneurial capabilities to succeed in the modern workforce and contribute positively to society.

Our Goals

Institutional Objectives

The objectives of Benchmark Business and Technical College are to:

  • Provide quality education and training in business, vocational, and technical fields.
  • Promote skills development and entrepreneurship among youths and adults.
  • Enhance employment opportunities and self-reliance among graduates.
  • Develop practical competencies aligned with labor market demands.
  • Foster innovation, professionalism, and ethical leadership among students.
  • Support community development through capacity building and skills training.
  • Build partnerships with government, private sector, and development organizations.
  • Ensure inclusive education opportunities for marginalized groups, including women and youth.
